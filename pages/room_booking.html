Booking Policies

  • Check-in / Check-out

    Check-in time: 3:00 PM | Check-out time: 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out can be arranged based on availability (additional charges may apply).

  • Reservation Guarantee

    A valid credit card is required to guarantee your reservation. Your card will not be charged until check-in unless specified in the rate terms.

  • Cancellation Policy

    Cancellations made 48 hours prior to arrival date will receive a full refund. Cancellations made within 48 hours of arrival will be charged one night's stay.

  • Children Policy

    Children under 12 years stay free when sharing a room with parents. Additional beds can be arranged for a fee of $50 per night.

  • Pet Policy

    Select rooms are pet-friendly with a non-refundable fee of $75 per stay. Please inform the hotel in advance if traveling with pets.
